Converters

PDF ↔ JPG converter

Convert a PDF page to a JPG image or turn an image (JPG/PNG) into a PDF file. Everything runs locally in your browser, with no files sent to any server.

Download converted file
Limitations: for now, the PDF → JPG conversion only processes the first page. The tool supports JPG and PNG images for conversion to PDF.

Fraction ↔ decimal converter

Enter a fraction like 1/3 or a decimal number like 0.75, and the tool converts to the other format. Useful for cooking, DIY or math exercises.

Tip: you can use comma or dot for decimals. Fractions are simplified (approximation).

Meters ↔ feet converter

Convert lengths between meters and feet. Useful for blueprints, construction or reading imperial-unit documentation.

Value used: 1 m = 3.28084 ft (approx.).

Speed converter m/s ↔ km/h

Simple conversion between meters per second (m/s) and kilometers per hour (km/h). Handy for weather, running or physics.

Formula: 1 m/s = 3.6 km/h.

Energy converter Joules ↔ kilocalories

Convert an energy value between joules (J) and kilocalories (kcal). Useful for nutrition labels, sport or physics.

Value used: 1 kcal = 4,184 J (“food calorie”).

Pressure converter bar ↔ psi

Convert a pressure between bar and psi, for tyres, hydraulic systems or compressors.

Value used: 1 bar ≈ 14.5038 psi.

Time converter: days → hours / minutes / seconds

Enter a number of days to get the equivalent in hours, minutes and seconds. Useful to quickly understand project duration, holidays or deadlines.

1 day = 24 hours = 1,440 minutes = 86,400 seconds.

KB / MB / GB / TB converter

Enter a size and its starting unit (bytes, KB, MB, GB or TB) to get the equivalents in all other units. Perfect to quickly understand how large a file really is.

Conversions use the common binary units in computing: 1 KB = 1024 bytes, 1 MB = 1024 KB, and so on.

Currency converter (manual rate)

Enter an amount, choose the conversion direction and set your own rate, for example: 1 € = 1.08 $. No API call, everything is calculated locally in your browser.

Example: with a rate of 1 € = 1.08 $, 100 € becomes 108 $. The other way around, 108 $ at this rate equals 100 €.

Flow rate converter L/min ↔ m³/h

Enter a flow rate in litres per minute (L/min) or in cubic meters per hour (m³/h) to get the other value. Useful for water networks, pumps, irrigation, etc.

Formulas used: m³/h = L/min × 0.06  •  L/min ≈ m³/h × 16.67.

Surface → volume converter (m² ↔ m³)

Enter a surface in m² and a height in meters to get the volume in m³. Useful to estimate concrete, paint or the usable volume of a room.

Formula used: volume (m³) = surface (m²) × height (m).

Distance converter km ↔ miles

Simple and immediate conversion. 1 km ≈ 0.621371 mile. All calculations are done directly in your browser.

Note: this converter uses the standard value 1 km = 0.621371 mile.

Need another kind of tool? Also explore security (passwords), health (BMI) or organization & misc.

°C ↔ °F converter

Fast conversion between Celsius and Fahrenheit for recipes, weather, or data sheets.

Formulas used: °F = °C × 9/5 + 32 and °C = (°F − 32) × 5/9.

Percentage calculator

Quickly calculate a percentage discount or increase (sales, price rise, discount, etc.).

Example: 100 € with a 20% discount → 80 €. 100 € with a 20% increase → 120 €.

Weight converter kg ↔ lb

Simple conversion between kilograms and pounds. All calculations are done directly in your browser.

Value used: 1 kg = 2.20462 lb (approx.).

Length converter cm ↔ inches

Convert centimeters to inches and back. 1 inch = 2.54 cm.

Conversion used: 1 in = 2.54 cm (standard value).

Volume converter liters ↔ gallons (US)

Convert liters to US gallons and the other way around. 1 L ≈ 0.264172 gallon (US).

This tool uses the US gallon: 1 L ≈ 0.264172 gal (US).

Time converter hours ↔ minutes

Convert hours to minutes or minutes to hours. Fill one of the fields, the other will be computed.

1 hour = 60 minutes. You can use decimals (e.g. 1.5 h).

Speed converter km/h ↔ mph

Convert a speed between kilometers per hour (km/h) and miles per hour (mph). Helpful for driving abroad or reading foreign specs.

Conversion used: 1 mph ≈ 1.60934 km/h.

About Universe Tools
