Converters

Concrete calculator (volume + cement bags)

This tool helps you estimate the concrete volume in m³ for a rectangular slab, and the number of cement bags required using a standard mix (around 350 kg of cement per m³ of concrete).

Approximation using a typical mix of ≈ 350 kg cement per m³ of concrete. This does not account for project specific conditions (reinforcement, additives, losses, weather, etc.). For big projects, always confirm with a professional.
Energy converter: Joules ↔ calories ↔ kWh

Enter a value in Joules, in food calories (kcal) or in kilowatt-hours (kWh) and the converter will automatically compute the other two units. Conversion used: 1 kcal = 4,184 J, 1 kWh = 3,600,000 J.

Reminder: 1 kcal ≈ 4,184 J (food calorie) and 1 kWh = 3,600,000 J. Values are rounded for readability.
Fraction ↔ Decimal ↔ Percentage converter

Enter a fraction (e.g. 1/4), a decimal (e.g. 0.25) or a percentage (e.g. 25) and the converter will compute the two other forms automatically.

Example: 1/4 → 0.25 → 25 %. For decimals, an approximate fraction is computed (denominator ≤ 100).
Angle converter: degrees ↔ % slope ↔ radians

Convert any angle instantly between degrees, slope percentage and radians. All calculations happen locally in your browser.

Formulas:
• % slope = tan(°) × 100
• radians = degrees × π/180
• degrees = radians × 180/π
Lumens ↔ Watts (LED)

This converter gives you an approximate LED wattage from a lumen value, and the other way around. It uses an average efficiency of ≈ 90 lumens per watt, which may vary depending on the LED quality.

Example: an 800 lm LED bulb is roughly 8.9 W with 90 lm/W efficiency. Real values depend on each LED model.
Travel time calculator

Estimate how long it takes to cover a given distance at a given average speed. Useful for car trips, cycling or project planning.

This is an ideal estimate (constant speed, no stops, no traffic). 1 mile ≈ 1.609 km.
Decimal hours ↔ hours + minutes

Convert decimal hours (e.g. 7.5 h) to hours + minutes (7 h 30), or the other way around. Useful for timesheets, payroll or project tracking.

Examples: 7.5 h → 7 h 30 • 1.25 h → 1 h 15 7 h 30 → 7.50 h • 1 h 15 → 1.25 h. Simple rounding to the nearest minute.
Euro ↔ Bitcoin / Crypto converter (manual rate)

Enter an amount in euros, the price of 1 unit of Bitcoin or any other crypto, then choose the conversion direction. No API is used, everything runs locally.

Example: if 1 BTC = €65,000, then €100 ≈ 0.00154 BTC. This is an indicative converter and does not replace an exchange platform.
PDF ↔ JPG converter

Convert a PDF page to a JPG image or turn an image (JPG/PNG) into a PDF file. Everything runs locally in your browser, with no files sent to any server.

Limitations: for now, the PDF → JPG conversion only processes the first page. The tool supports JPG and PNG images for conversion to PDF.

Tip: you can use comma or dot for decimals. Fractions are simplified (approximation).

Formulas used: m³/h = L/min × 0.06  •  L/min ≈ m³/h × 16.67.

Formula used: volume (m³) = surface (m²) × height (m).

Enter the loan amount, annual interest rate and duration in years. This tool generates a yearly amortization table using either linear amortization (constant principal) or a declining balance with constant payments (standard amortizing loan).

Rental yield calculator

Enter the purchase price, the monthly rent and yearly expenses (property tax, HOA fees, maintenance…). The tool computes gross yield and net yield.

Formulas used:
• Gross yield = (yearly rent / purchase price) × 100
• Net yield ≈ ((yearly rent − yearly expenses) / purchase price) × 100
Electricity consumption W → kWh → €

Estimate the electricity consumption of a device from its power in watts, usage time and kWh price. Everything is calculated locally in your browser.

Example: a 1,000 W device used 2 h/day for 30 days at 0.20 €/kWh consumes ≈ 60 kWh, i.e. ≈ 12 €.

Target heart rate (training)

This tool estimates your target heart rate for exercise based on your age, using the simple formula 220 − age for maximum heart rate. Results are indicative only and do not replace medical advice.

Typical adult guidelines: moderate zone ≈ 50–70 % of max HR, intense zone ≈ 70–85 % of max HR.
These values are approximations and do not replace a medical opinion or a supervised stress test.
BMI calculator (Body Mass Index)

Calculate your BMI based on your height and weight. This gives a general indication, but does not replace medical advice.
