About Universe Tools

Universe Tools is a free online toolbox designed to help you convert, calculate, generate and automate simple everyday tasks. All computations run directly in your browser, with no data stored or transmitted to a server.

The site offers 60+ tools grouped into several categories: converters (km ↔ miles, kg ↔ lb, °C ↔ °F, cm ↔ inches, liters ↔ gallons, days ↔ hours / minutes / seconds, KB / MB / GB / TB, manual currency converter, flow rate L/min ↔ m³/h, area ↔ volume), finance & budget (savings goal simulator, monthly savings simulator with compound interest, monthly budget calculator, bill splitter, loan monthly payment calculator, VAT calculator, net ↔ gross salary), security (password generator), health (BMI calculator) and organization & misc (stopwatch & timer, countdown in days, random picker, age calculator, fake data generator, average calculator, etc.).

All tools are free to use, require no sign-up, and are built to save you a few seconds each time: instead of searching for a different site for every task, you keep Universe Tools in your bookmarks and access all your everyday tools from a single page.
