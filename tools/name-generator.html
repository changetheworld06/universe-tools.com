About this Name Generator

Finding a strong name can be surprisingly hard. Whether you are naming a business, a brand, a product, or looking for a baby name, you usually want something that is easy to remember, easy to pronounce, and consistent with the “feel” you are aiming for. This Name Generator helps you explore name ideas by mixing your keywords with different structures: combined words, short variants, prefixes, suffixes, and “brandable” syllable-based names.

Start with a few keywords (for example your industry, your values, or a theme). Then choose a type: for business and brands, the tool can propose names that include common endings (like “Labs”, “Studio”, or “Solutions”). For baby names, it uses a different set of seeds and variations. The style affects the suggestions: “Tech” tends to generate more modern, sharp names; “Luxury” leans into elegant sounds and premium cues; “Cute” aims for friendly and playful results. The “language feel” option adjusts the syllables and endings to produce names that sound more English-ish, French-ish, Latin-ish, or Nordic-ish.

The “max length” filter is especially useful for brandability: short names often work better in logos, domains, and social handles. However, you should always verify real-world availability. This tool does not call any external API and does not check trademark databases. It simply gives you ideas quickly, then provides convenient shortcuts to run your own searches.

Everything runs locally in your browser. Your keywords and choices are not uploaded or stored. Use the generator to iterate: change one parameter at a time (style, language feel, max length), generate multiple batches, and shortlist the best options. Once you have a shortlist, test pronunciation, check meaning in other languages, and validate domain and trademark availability.

Tip: for brand names, try 2–3 strong keywords and keep max length at 8–12. For baby names, try themes like nature, sky, ocean, strength, or calm.
