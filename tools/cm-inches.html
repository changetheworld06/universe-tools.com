About this centimeters to inches converter (cm ↔ in)

This free centimeters to inches converter helps you switch quickly between the metric system (centimeters) and the imperial system (inches). It’s useful in many everyday situations: measuring a screen size, checking furniture dimensions, comparing clothing or shoe size charts, reading an international product listing, working on DIY tasks, or understanding specifications in technical documentation. Because different countries and industries use different measurement conventions, it’s common to encounter values written in inches even if you personally think in centimeters (or the opposite).

The conversion itself is simple and reliable because it is based on an exact definition: 1 inch equals exactly 2.54 centimeters. That means you can convert from centimeters to inches by dividing by 2.54, and convert from inches to centimeters by multiplying by 2.54. For example, 10 cm is 10 ÷ 2.54 = 3.9370 inches, and 5 inches is 5 × 2.54 = 12.7 cm. This tool performs the calculation instantly and displays a clear result, so you don’t have to do mental math or search for a conversion table.

The converter is designed to be practical: you can type in either field and the other field updates automatically. This is convenient when you’re copying measurements from a website or a spec sheet and you want to sanity-check sizes quickly (for example, a laptop screen advertised as 15.6 inches, or a piece of wood listed in inches while your tape measure is in centimeters). You can also use it for precision work because the tool supports decimals and keeps enough digits for accurate conversions.

All calculations run locally in your browser. There is no account, no upload, and your numbers are not stored by the page. If you need fast unit conversions as part of your workflow, you can bookmark this page and reuse it anytime on desktop or mobile.

Tip: if you are converting display sizes, remember that “inches” usually refer to the diagonal measurement.
