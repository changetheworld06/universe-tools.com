About this area to volume converter (m² to m³)

This free area to volume converter helps you compute a volume in m³ from a surface area in m² and a height / thickness in meters. It’s useful for many real-world projects: estimating concrete volume for a slab, screed or foundation, calculating soil or gravel needs for landscaping, sizing insulation layers, and approximating storage or fill volumes.

The formula is simple: volume (m³) = area (m²) × height (m). If your thickness is given in centimeters, convert it to meters first (for example, 10 cm = 0.10 m). This tool helps you avoid mistakes when converting units and lets you test multiple scenarios quickly.

All calculations run locally in your browser. No account, no upload, and no data is sent. The result is shown instantly so you can plan quantities before purchasing materials or booking transport.

Tip: if you want to estimate bags or tonnage, you also need the material density (kg/m³), which depends on the product.

This calculator provides a basic geometric volume. It does not account for irregular shapes, compaction, or material density. Additional calculations may be required to estimate weight or number of bags.
