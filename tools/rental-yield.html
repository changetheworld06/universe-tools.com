Calculate gross and net rental yield (local & private)

Rental yield helps you compare property investments by relating annual rental income to the purchase price. Gross yield uses rent only, while net yield subtracts yearly expenses such as insurance, taxes, HOA fees and maintenance.

This tool runs locally in your browser, so your numbers are not uploaded to a server. Use it to quickly benchmark deals before doing a full cashflow analysis.
