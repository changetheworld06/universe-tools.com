About this meters to feet converter

Meters (m) are the standard unit of length in the metric system and are used in most countries for everyday measurements and technical documents. Feet (ft) are part of the imperial / US customary system and are still common in the United States and in some industries worldwide. If you read construction plans, real estate listings, product specs, architecture drawings, or international engineering references, you may need to convert between meters and feet to avoid misinterpretation.

This calculator converts meters to feet and feet to meters instantly using the exact conversion factor: 1 meter = 3.28084 feet. To convert meters to feet, multiply by 3.28084. To convert feet to meters, divide by 3.28084. The tool applies these operations automatically and shows a clear result you can copy.

To use it, type a value in one field (meters or feet) and click “Convert”. The other field will be filled automatically. If both fields are filled, the tool prioritizes the meters field, so it’s best to clear one field before converting.

Everything runs locally in your browser: no upload, no server processing, and no external API calls. Results are provided instantly. For critical work (engineering tolerances, compliance documents), always confirm the required rounding rules (for example, 2 vs 3 decimals) depending on your standard or project specifications.
