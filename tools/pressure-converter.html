Bar to PSI conversion explained

This tool converts pressure units between bar and psi (pounds per square inch). It is helpful for checking tire pressure, air compressors, pressure washers, and hydraulic systems. The calculation is performed locally in your browser.

To convert bar to psi, the formula is: psi = bar × 14.5038. To convert psi to bar: bar = psi ÷ 14.5038.
