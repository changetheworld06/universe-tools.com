About kilograms and pounds conversion

Kilograms (kg) and pounds (lb) are two common units used to measure mass (and in everyday language, “weight”). The kilogram is the standard unit in the International System of Units (SI) and is used in most countries for personal weight, food packaging, shipping labels, and technical measurements. Pounds are commonly used in the United States and still appear in many contexts such as fitness tracking, product specifications, and travel luggage limits. Because both units are widespread, quick and accurate conversions are useful in daily life.

This free converter lets you type a value in either unit and instantly see the equivalent in the other unit. For example, if you track your body weight in kilograms but a fitness app or program expects pounds, you can convert in one step. The same applies to parcel shipping: some carriers or marketplaces show limits in pounds, while your scale might show kilograms.

The conversion factors used here are the standard definitions: 1 kilogram = 2.2046226218 pounds and 1 pound = 0.45359237 kilograms. Results are rounded to two decimals for readability. If you need higher precision, you can increase the displayed decimals in the script.

Privacy is a key principle of Universe Tools: everything is computed locally in your browser. No data is uploaded, stored, or shared.

Tip: For luggage limits or shipping, consider rounding up slightly (depending on your carrier’s rules).
