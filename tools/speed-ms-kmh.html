About this m/s ↔ km/h converter

Speed can be expressed in different units depending on the domain. In everyday life and road traffic, most countries use kilometers per hour (km/h). In science, engineering, and many weather datasets, speed is frequently given in meters per second (m/s). If you read wind forecasts, analyze sports timing, compare sprint speeds, or work with physics formulas, you often need a quick and reliable conversion between these two units.

The relationship is based on time and distance definitions: 1 hour = 3600 seconds and 1 kilometer = 1000 meters. That leads to a simple factor: 1 m/s = 3.6 km/h. To convert m/s → km/h, multiply by 3.6. To convert km/h → m/s, divide by 3.6. This tool applies the standard factor and displays a clear result with two decimals, which is typically enough for real-world use (wind speed, running, cycling, quick technical checks).

Example: 10 m/s corresponds to 36 km/h. A runner at 5 m/s is moving at about 18 km/h. A strong wind of 20 m/s is around 72 km/h. These comparisons become intuitive once you can convert instantly.

Privacy is a key principle of Universe Tools: the conversion is performed 100% locally in your browser. No data is uploaded, stored, or shared.

Tip: if you just need a mental estimate, km/h ≈ m/s × 3.6 (exact), or m/s ≈ km/h ÷ 3.6.
