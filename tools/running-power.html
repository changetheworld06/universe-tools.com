Running Power Zones

Calculate your running training zones from your FTP (critical power). Zones are expressed as power ranges in watts.

Enter your running FTP (critical power) in watts. The calculator estimates common training zones (easy, endurance, tempo, threshold, intervals) as % of FTP. Use this for guidance only.

Your best ~30–60 min power, or the value from your watch/platform.
Round values to the nearest watt (1, 5, 10, …).
Example ranges used here (as % of FTP): Z1 <80%, Z2 80–90%, Z3 90–100%, Z4 100–110%, Z5 110–130%. Different platforms/coaches may use different definitions.

Running power zones from FTP (local & private)

Running power zones help structure training by turning your FTP (also called critical power) into watt ranges. They can be used for easy runs, endurance, tempo workouts, threshold sessions, and higher-intensity intervals.

This calculator runs fully in your browser. Your FTP value is not uploaded, which keeps your data private. If your watch or app uses different zone boundaries, adjust the results accordingly.
