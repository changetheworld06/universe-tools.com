How to convert km to miles

The standard conversion is: 1 kilometer = 0.621371 miles. To convert kilometers to miles, multiply by 0.621371.

For the reverse conversion: 1 mile = 1.60934 kilometers. This tool converts both ways instantly and runs locally in your browser.
