About this bill splitter

This bill splitter helps you calculate how much each person should pay when sharing a restaurant bill, drinks, takeaway, or any group expense. Enter the total amount, the number of people, and optionally a tip percentage.

The tool computes the tip from the bill total, adds it to the grand total, then splits everything equally to give a clear per-person amount. It’s perfect for quick checks when traveling, dining with friends, or organizing shared costs.

Everything runs locally in your browser: no login, no tracking of your values, and no data sent to a server. For currencies other than euros, you can still use the same calculator—just enter the numbers in your currency.

Tip rules vary by country and venue. Always check whether service is already included before adding a tip.
