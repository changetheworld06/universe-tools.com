About this hours ↔ minutes converter

Hours and minutes are both standard units for measuring time, but depending on the situation you may need one format instead of the other. Work schedules, timesheets and invoices often use hours (for example, 1.75 h), while cooking timers, sports workouts, meeting durations, and travel segments are usually expressed in minutes (for example, 105 min). This tool converts between hours (h) and minutes (min) instantly, so you can move from one format to the other without manual calculations.

The rule is simple: 1 hour = 60 minutes. To convert hours to minutes, multiply by 60. For example, 2 hours becomes 120 minutes, and 1.5 hours becomes 90 minutes. To convert minutes to hours, divide by 60. For example, 45 minutes becomes 0.75 hours, and 90 minutes becomes 1.50 hours. This converter applies those operations automatically and displays a readable result.

To use it, enter a value in one field (either hours or minutes) and click “Convert”. The other field will be filled automatically. If both fields are filled, the converter will prioritize the hours field, so the best practice is to clear one field before converting. This makes the tool fast for daily use and avoids mistakes when you are copying numbers from another document.

Everything runs locally in your browser. This page does not send your inputs to any server and does not call external APIs. It’s designed for quick conversions on desktop or mobile, whether you’re preparing an invoice, planning a workout session, checking study time, or converting travel durations.

Tip: if you need higher precision for hours, minutes divided by 60 may produce decimals (example: 1 minute = 0.0167 hours). For billing, keep the precision rule required by your employer or client.
