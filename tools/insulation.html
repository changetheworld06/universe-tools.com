Insulation calculator (R, U → thickness)

Compute insulation thickness from R-value, U-value or thermal conductivity λ. Useful for quick building physics checks. Results are indicative only.

Choose what you know (R-value or U-value), enter the thermal conductivity λ of the material, and the tool will estimate the required insulation thickness.

R = thermal resistance, U = thermal transmittance.
λ depends on the material (mineral wool, PIR, EPS, wood, etc.).
