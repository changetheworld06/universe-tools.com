VAT calculator (net ↔ gross)

Calculate VAT, net price (excluding tax) or gross price (including tax) with any VAT rate. Local calculation only.

Enter a VAT rate and either a net price or a gross price. The other values are calculated automatically.

VAT amount = Gross price − Net price.

How VAT calculations work

VAT (Value Added Tax) is added to the net price to obtain the gross price. This calculator helps you convert net ↔ gross using any VAT rate, and shows the VAT amount separately. Useful for invoices, quotes, budgeting, and quick price comparisons.

  • Gross = Net × (1 + rate)
  • Net = Gross ÷ (1 + rate)
  • VAT = Gross − Net
