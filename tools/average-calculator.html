About this average calculator (mean)

This free average calculator computes the mean of a list of numbers. It is useful for grades, budgets, sports statistics, measurements, and quick data checks. Paste your numbers separated by commas or spaces, then click the button to get the result instantly.

The mean is calculated with the standard formula: average = (sum of values) ÷ (number of values). For example, the average of 10, 20 and 30 is (10 + 20 + 30) ÷ 3 = 20.

Everything runs locally in your browser. No account, no upload, and no data is sent. The tool simply parses your input, keeps valid numbers, sums them, and divides by the count.

Tip: if you need a weighted average (different coefficients), you need a weighted-mean calculator.

What is this average calculator used for?

This average calculator is used to compute the mean value of a list of numbers. It provides a quick and reliable way to summarize numerical data with a single representative value, commonly called the average.

  • Professionals analyzing prices, metrics, or performance indicators
  • Individuals comparing budgets, expenses, or measurements

Concrete examples

  • Calculating the average of several exam scores
  • Finding the mean price of a list of products
  • Computing average measurements from repeated tests

Common mistakes to avoid

  • ❌ Mixing non-numeric values with numbers
  • ❌ Forgetting to include all values in the calculation
  • ❌ Confusing a simple average with a weighted average

This calculator automatically ignores invalid entries, but the result depends on providing a complete and correct list of values.

Limits and possible alternatives

This tool calculates a simple arithmetic mean. It does not support weighted averages, medians, or statistical dispersion such as variance or standard deviation.
