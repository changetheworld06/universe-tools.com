About this average calculator (mean)

This free average calculator computes the mean of a list of numbers. It is useful for grades, budgets, sports statistics, measurements, and quick data checks. Paste your numbers separated by commas or spaces, then click the button to get the result instantly.

The mean is calculated with the standard formula: average = (sum of values) ÷ (number of values). For example, the average of 10, 20 and 30 is (10 + 20 + 30) ÷ 3 = 20.

Everything runs locally in your browser. No account, no upload, and no data is sent. The tool simply parses your input, keeps valid numbers, sums them, and divides by the count.

Tip: if you need a weighted average (different coefficients), you need a weighted-mean calculator.

Example: (10 + 20 + 30) / 3 = 20.
