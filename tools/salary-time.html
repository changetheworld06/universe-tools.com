About this salary converter

Salary numbers are often presented in different formats depending on the country, the contract type, and the hiring context. Some job offers show an annual gross salary, others highlight a monthly pay, and hourly jobs commonly use an hourly wage. This free salary converter helps you translate those values into a comparable format so you can evaluate offers, budgets, or freelance rates more easily. Enter your weekly working hours and at least one salary value (annual, monthly, or hourly) and the tool computes the other two values instantly.

The conversion is based on simple calendar assumptions: 52 weeks per year and 12 months per year. From an annual salary, monthly salary is computed by dividing by 12. From annual salary and weekly working hours, hourly wage is computed by dividing annual salary by (hours per week × 52). If you provide monthly salary instead, annual salary is estimated as monthly × 12. If you provide hourly wage, annual salary is estimated as hourly × hours per week × 52.

This calculator is useful when comparing full-time vs part-time jobs, when negotiating compensation, or when planning personal finance. Keep in mind that real-life payslips may include taxes, employer contributions, bonuses, paid time off rules, bank rounding, or specific regional practices. Those details can change the final “net” amount you receive. Still, this tool gives a quick and consistent estimate that is helpful for comparisons.

Privacy is a key principle of Universe Tools: everything is computed locally in your browser. No data is uploaded, stored, or shared.

Tip: if you are converting hourly wages, double-check your weekly hours (35, 37.5, 40…) to get realistic results.

Assumes 52 weeks/year and 12 months/year. Results are indicative.
