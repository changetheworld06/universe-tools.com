About this liters to US gallons converter

Liters (L) are the standard unit of volume in the metric system and are used in most countries for everyday measurements such as beverages, fuel, water tanks, and container capacity. In the United States, many labels, specifications, and instructions use US gallons (gal) instead. If you read product packaging, fuel economy references, pool or aquarium volumes, or any US-based technical documentation, converting between liters and US gallons becomes necessary to avoid mistakes.

This tool converts liters to US gallons and US gallons to liters instantly using the standard conversion factor: 1 liter = 0.264172 US gallons. To convert liters to US gallons, multiply by 0.264172. To convert US gallons to liters, divide by 0.264172 (or multiply by 3.78541). The calculator applies these operations automatically and shows a clear result you can copy.

To use it, enter a value in one field (either liters or gallons) and click “Convert”. The other field will be filled automatically. If both fields are filled, the tool prioritizes the liters field, so it’s best to clear one field before converting.

Note that US gallons are different from UK (imperial) gallons. A UK gallon is larger, so conversions will not match if you use the wrong unit. This page converts liters ↔ US gallons only. Everything runs locally in your browser: no upload, no server processing, and no external API calls.

Tip: for everyday use, 1 gallon (US) ≈ 3.785 liters. For precise work, rely on the full factor used by this tool.
