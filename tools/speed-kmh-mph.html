About this km/h ↔ mph converter

Speed units can be confusing when you travel, buy a vehicle, read international technical specs, or compare sports results. Most countries use kilometers per hour (km/h), while the United States and the United Kingdom commonly use miles per hour (mph) for road speed limits and many everyday contexts. This free converter helps you switch between km/h and mph instantly, without any download or account. You simply enter a number in one field and click convert to get the equivalent value in the other unit.

The conversion is based on the standard relationship between miles and kilometers: 1 mile = 1.60934 kilometers. From that, converting is straightforward: to convert km/h → mph, divide by 1.60934; to convert mph → km/h, multiply by 1.60934. This tool applies that factor and returns a clear result with two decimal places, which is usually precise enough for real-world use (driving, running pace checks, quick comparisons, and general planning).

Typical examples: 50 km/h is about 31.07 mph (a common city limit in many places). 70 mph is about 112.65 km/h (a typical highway speed in the US). If you are renting a car abroad, reading a dashboard display, using a GPS, or watching a motorsport broadcast, having a fast conversion helps avoid mistakes and makes the numbers intuitive.

Like the other pages on Universe Tools, this converter runs 100% locally in your browser. Your input values are not sent to any server and are not stored. It’s quick, private, and works on mobile and desktop.

Tip: if you frequently convert speed limits, remember the shortcut: mph ≈ km/h ÷ 1.6 (rough estimate), then use this tool for exact values.
